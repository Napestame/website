Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. It's a prestigous school that places a lot of value on rigor, liberal arts, experiential learning, and other ambitious values. It also balances strog support of modern innovation with the maintainence of old traditions. To get in, you neeed to excel in academics already and even then, a lot of the coursework will still be challenging. The university also favors majors in medicine and the sciences.
